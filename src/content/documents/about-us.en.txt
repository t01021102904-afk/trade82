About Trade82
Trade82 is a global B2B marketplace connecting Korean suppliers with buyers around the world.
Many Korean businesses offer competitive products but struggle to reach reliable overseas buyers. At the same time, international buyers often find it difficult to discover Korean products, compare suppliers, and communicate directly with the right companies.
Trade82 was created to make that process simpler.
Buyers can explore Korean products, submit sourcing requests, and communicate directly with suppliers. Sellers can present their products to international buyers, respond to real demand, and discover new export opportunities.
Our Mission
Our mission is to reduce the distance between Korean suppliers and global buyers.
We believe international trade should not be limited to large companies with extensive networks and resources. Smaller brands, manufacturers, distributors, and emerging businesses should also have a practical way to reach international markets.
What We Do
Trade82 helps global buyers:
• Discover products from Korean suppliers
• Submit RFQs based on specific sourcing requirements
• Compare products and trade conditions
• Communicate directly with potential suppliers
• Request additional sourcing support when needed
Trade82 helps Korean sellers:
• Introduce their products to international buyers
• Receive inquiries and RFQs from overseas markets
• Communicate directly with potential business partners
• Build new export relationships
• Expand into markets they may not have reached before
Why Trade82
Trade82 focuses on real connections between buyers and sellers.
Instead of making businesses search across multiple websites, directories, and communication channels, we provide one place to discover products, submit requests, discuss terms, and move potential transactions forward.
We are building Trade82 for businesses that want practical opportunities, clear communication, and long-term international partnerships.
Our Vision
Our vision is to become a trusted starting point for discovering and sourcing Korean products globally.
We want a buyer searching for a Korean product to know where to begin, and a Korean seller preparing to export to know where to meet potential buyers.
Trade82 connects global demand with Korean supply.
